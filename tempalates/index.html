Customer Churn Prediction

Customer churn prediction refers to the process of identifying and predicting which customers are likely to stop using a company's products or services in the near future. It is a crucial aspect of customer relationship management (CRM) and is particularly important for businesses that rely on subscription-based models or long-term customer relationships. Customer churn, also known as customer attrition, can have a significant negative impact on a company's revenue and profitability.
